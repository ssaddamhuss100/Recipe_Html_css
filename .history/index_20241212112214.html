Gulam Jamun

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Bread
  • Oil

Recipe

  1. Prepare the syrup by boiling sugar and water.
  2. Fry the gulab jamuns until golden brown.
  3. Soak the fried balls in the warm syrup for 30 minutes.
  4. Serve and enjoy your Gulab Jamun!

Barfi Jamun

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Milk Powder
  • Cardamom

Recipe

  1. Make a dough using milk powder and sugar.
  2. Shape the dough into small balls and fry.
  3. Dip the fried balls in a flavored syrup.
  4. Let them cool before serving!
